Penn State Harrisburg Virtual Tour

Globe Fountain

Hello and welcome to Penn State Harrisburg, an undergraduate college and graduate school of the Pennsylvania State University, one of the largest and most widely recognized institutions in the nation. Located in Middletown, just eight miles east of Harrisburg, the college boasts nationally-accredited programs, award-winning faculty who are accomplished teachers and scholars, and the resources of a world-class research university.

We begin our tour at the globe fountain, just in front of the Student Enrichment Center, which we’ll go inside in a later tour stop. This sculpture reminds us that Penn State Harrisburg is truly a global campus: we welcome students, faculty and staff from around the country and around the world, and our international programs have taken our students to many places abroad.

We invite you to look around in this 360 degree environment and when you’re ready, follow the numbered tour stops to get a fuller picture of our beautiful campus. You can also use the “Navigate to…” menu to jump to any stop on the tour.
